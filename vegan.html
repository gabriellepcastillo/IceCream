VAN LEEUWEN

This ice cream shop carries various of vegan flavors using
their house-made cashew milk, organic coconut milk, organic
extra virgin coconut oil, organic cane sugar, pure cocoa butter,
and organic carob bean. All resulting in a devious vegan ice cream.

TAIYAKI

In the heart of China Town this small ice cream shop gives a great
variety of flavor. They offer a whole vegan line which includes
coconut m ilk soft serve, vegan match, and vegan frappe. This
shop used almond, oat, and coconut milk as their bases.

WHIPPED

This ice cream shop is also a lovely experience. Not only is
it entirely plant based and 100% vegan, but they also ship
nationally. This store is located in the heart of SoHo and be sure
to get there early because there is a long line.
