MORGANSTERN’S

We have all seen odd flavors of Ice Cream but Morgenstern’s,
the contemporary ice cream parlor, has Szechuan Peppercorn
chocolate. This flavor definitely hits the spicy and sweet genre.

ODD FELLOWS

This ice cream shop holds the most wacky and weird flavors
I have ever heard of, along with the classic flavors of course.
On top of all of the yummy fun flavors they also have vegan
options. They are located in Brooklyn NY.

CHINA TOWN ICE CREAM FACTORY

This shop is in the heart of chinatown they offer many delicious
flavors. However they are on the top list for odd flavors with
their Black Sesame flavor which is black sesame ice cream with
black sesame seeds.
