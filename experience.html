CATCH'N ICE CREAM

This ice cream shop does not just sell ice cream balls but it is
a 360 ice cream experience. The owner and creator Dylan
Lemay is a TikTok star with over 15 millions followers. The focus
on the store is to create a unique experience where you
actually catch your own ice cream.

UES

Did you know this ice cream shop does not just sell ice cream?!
Once you enter it may look normal but, If you utter a secret
phrase you will be let into their secret speakeasy. If
you get in you will be time traveled back to the prohibition era.
Keep in mind this is only for 21 and older.

ICE CREAM MUSEUM

Enter an immersive experience where you walk through pure
imagination. From swimming in a pool full of sprinkles to walking
through various galleries. This experience is definitely not like
any you have seen before.
